# HR Policies Knowledge Base

## Time Off & Leave
Employees receive 20 days PTO per year (accrued monthly).
Sick leave: 10 days per year, does not roll over.
Submit PTO requests via the HR portal at least 5 business days in advance.
Bereavement leave: 3 days for immediate family, 1 day for extended family.
Parental leave: 16 weeks fully paid (primary caregiver), 6 weeks (secondary caregiver).

## Payroll & Compensation
Payroll is processed bi-weekly, deposited on Fridays.
Payslips are available in the HR portal under "Compensation".
For payroll errors or missing payments, contact payroll@company.com within 5 business days.
Annual salary reviews happen in January. Merit increases are effective February 1st.

## Benefits
Health insurance enrollment happens during onboarding and open enrollment (October annually).
Benefits include: medical, dental, vision, 401(k) with 4% company match, life insurance.
To add or remove dependents, submit a qualifying life event form within 30 days of the event.
FSA and HSA enrollment is available during open enrollment.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote positions require a home office stipend form ($500 one-time, $50/month ongoing).
All remote work must be done on company-approved devices with VPN enabled.

## Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year: mid-year (July) and annual (January).
Goals are set at the beginning of each year via the goal-setting tool in the HR portal.
Employees rated "Below Expectations" receive a 60-day Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).

## Onboarding
New employees complete a 30-day onboarding program.
Onboarding includes: HR orientation, IT setup, team introduction, compliance training.
Buddy program pairs new hires with an experienced colleague for the first 90 days.

## Code of Conduct
All employees must complete annual ethics and compliance training.
Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation are grounds for immediate termination.
Report concerns via the anonymous ethics hotline at ethics@company.com or 1-800-555-0100.

## Expense Reimbursement
Expenses under $50 can be submitted directly; over $50 requires manager pre-approval.
Submit expense reports monthly via the finance portal with receipts.
Reimbursements are processed within 10 business days.
