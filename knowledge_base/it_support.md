# IT Support Knowledge Base

## Password Reset
To reset your password, visit the internal portal at https://it.company.com/reset or contact the IT helpdesk at it-support@company.com.
Passwords must be at least 12 characters and changed every 90 days.
Response time: Same day for business-critical accounts, 24 hours for standard accounts.

## VPN Access
VPN is required for all remote work. Download the client from https://it.company.com/vpn.
If VPN is not connecting, try: (1) restart the app, (2) check your internet connection, (3) ensure 2FA is set up.
For new VPN access requests, submit a ticket with your manager's approval.

## Software Installation
All software installations require IT approval. Submit a request with business justification.
Licensed software list: Office 365, Slack, Zoom, Figma, GitHub, VS Code, JetBrains IDEs.
Unlicensed software will be removed during quarterly security audits.

## Hardware Issues
For broken or malfunctioning hardware, submit a ticket with photos if possible.
Laptops under warranty are replaced within 3 business days.
Loaner devices available from IT room 204 while repairs are pending.

## Email Configuration
Company email is hosted on Google Workspace. Access at mail.company.com.
Email aliases can be requested for project teams or departments.
Maximum attachment size: 25MB. Use Google Drive links for larger files.

## Security Incidents
Report any suspected phishing, malware, or security breach immediately to security@company.com.
Do NOT click suspicious links or open unexpected attachments.
IT will never ask for your password over email or chat.

## New Employee Setup
New employees receive a setup checklist on day one via email.
Standard setup: laptop provisioning (2 hours), account creation (same day), VPN access (same day), badge access (1 business day).

## Offboarding
IT receives automatic notification from HR when an employee is offboarded.
All company devices must be returned on the last day of employment.
Account access is revoked within 1 hour of the official end time.
