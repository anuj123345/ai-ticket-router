# HR Policies — Acme Corp

## Health Insurance
Acme Corp provides comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance for all full-time employees starting on day one. Employees may add spouses, domestic partners, and dependents to their plan. The company covers 80% of the premium for the employee and 50% for dependents.

Open enrollment is held every November for changes effective January 1. Life events (marriage, birth, divorce) allow mid-year changes within 30 days of the event.

## Mental Health Benefits
All employees have access to 12 free therapy or counseling sessions per year through our Employee Assistance Program (EAP). Sessions are confidential and can be booked at therapy.acmecorp.com. Mental health days are included within sick leave and do not require explanation.

## Retirement / 401(k)
Acme Corp offers a 401(k) plan with a 4% company match. Employees are eligible after 90 days of employment. Vesting of company contributions occurs over 3 years (33% per year). Enrollment can be done via the HR portal.

## Parental Leave
Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers receive 4 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Leave can begin up to 2 weeks before the expected due date. Adoption and surrogacy are covered under the same policy.

## Expense Reimbursement
Employees are reimbursed for approved business expenses. To submit expenses:
1. Keep all receipts (digital or physical)
2. Submit via the Expenses section of the HR portal within 30 days
3. Attach receipts and a brief description of the business purpose
4. Manager approval is required for expenses over $100
5. Reimbursements are processed in the next payroll cycle

Pre-approved expense categories: travel (flights, hotels, ground transport), client meals (up to $75/person), office supplies, professional development.

## Learning and Development
Employees receive a $1,500 annual learning and development (L&D) budget for courses, books, conferences, or certifications. Submit L&D requests through the HR portal before purchase. Reimbursement follows the standard expense process.

## Referral Bonus
Employees who refer a candidate who is hired and completes 90 days receive a $2,000 referral bonus. Submit referrals through the careers page using your employee ID.

## Disciplinary Policy
Performance or conduct issues follow a progressive process: verbal warning, written warning, performance improvement plan (PIP), and termination. Gross misconduct (fraud, harassment, data theft) may result in immediate termination without prior warnings.

## Grievance Process
If you have a workplace concern:
1. Attempt to resolve directly with the other party if safe to do so
2. Escalate to your manager
3. Contact HR at hr@acmecorp.com for formal grievance submission
4. All formal grievances are investigated within 10 business days
5. Anonymous reporting is available at acmecorp.ethicspoint.com

## Termination and Offboarding
On your last day, you must return all company equipment, revoke personal access to company systems, and complete the exit interview with HR. Final pay including accrued vacation is processed within 5 business days of the last working day.
