Cookie Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 12, 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They help the site remember information about your visit, such as your login session, so you don't have to re-enter credentials on every page.

How Solace Uses Cookies

Solace uses only strictly necessary cookies. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies that profile your behaviour across other websites.

Cookies We Set

Name Purpose Duration Type
session Maintains your authenticated login session so you don't need to sign in on every page visit. Session (cleared on browser close) Strictly Necessary
csrf_token Protects form submissions against cross-site request forgery attacks. Session Strictly Necessary

We do not set any persistent tracking cookies, and no cookie data is shared with advertising networks.

Third-Party Cookies

Solace does not load third-party scripts that set their own cookies. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts via a CDN link — Google may set its own cookies subject to Google's Privacy Policy, though we use the display=swap parameter to minimise unnecessary requests.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling the session cookie will prevent you from logging in to Solace. Below are links to cookie management guides for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Changes to This Policy

If we change the cookies we use, we will update this policy and the effective date above. Continued use of Solace after such changes constitutes acceptance.
