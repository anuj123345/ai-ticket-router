# IT Setup Guide — Acme Corp

## Getting Started
Your IT setup package will be delivered on your first day. It includes a laptop, accessories, and a welcome card with your temporary credentials.

## Laptop Setup
1. Power on your laptop and connect to the office Wi-Fi network: AcmeCorp-Staff (password provided by IT)
2. Log in with your temporary credentials (format: firstname.lastname@acmecorp.com)
3. Change your password immediately on first login — must be at least 12 characters with uppercase, number, and symbol
4. Enable FileVault (Mac) or BitLocker (Windows) disk encryption when prompted

## Required Software
The following software must be installed on day one:
- Slack (team communication)
- Zoom (video meetings)
- Google Chrome (primary browser)
- 1Password (password manager — license provided by IT)
- Notion (project documentation)

IT will pre-install these on managed devices. For bring-your-own-device, submit a request via the IT portal.

## Access Requests
To request access to a system or tool:
1. Log in to the IT portal at it.acmecorp.internal
2. Click "New Access Request"
3. Select the system and justify the business need
4. Your manager will be notified to approve
5. Access is typically provisioned within 1 business day

## Common Systems and Access
- Google Workspace: automatic on day one
- Slack: automatic on day one
- GitHub: request via IT portal, requires manager approval
- AWS / Cloud services: requires VP-level approval
- Customer data systems: requires Security team approval and GDPR training completion

## VPN
Remote access to internal systems requires VPN. Download the VPN client from the IT portal. Use your corporate credentials to connect. VPN must be enabled any time you access internal resources from outside the office.

## Security Policy
- Never share your password with anyone, including IT staff
- Lock your screen when stepping away (Cmd+Ctrl+Q on Mac, Win+L on Windows)
- Report any suspicious emails to security@acmecorp.com immediately
- Do not install unapproved software on company devices
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) is mandatory on all corporate accounts

## Equipment Issues
If your equipment is faulty or damaged, submit a ticket via the IT portal. Loaner equipment is available while repairs are processed. Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT and Security within 1 hour of discovery.
