# Finance & Procurement Knowledge Base

## Invoice Processing
Invoices should be submitted to accounts-payable@company.com with PO number in subject.
Standard payment terms: Net 30 from invoice date.
Invoices over $10,000 require CFO approval before processing.
For urgent payments, contact the AP team directly with justification.

## Purchase Orders
All purchases over $500 require a PO. Submit PO requests via the finance portal.
Software subscriptions: Annual contracts over $1,000 require IT security review.
Hardware purchases: Submit specs to IT for approval before purchasing.
Vendor onboarding takes 5–7 business days (compliance and banking verification).

## Budget & Forecasting
Department budgets are set annually in Q4 for the following year.
Mid-year budget adjustments require VP approval and finance review.
Q1/Q3 forecast reviews are mandatory for all department heads.
Budget tracking is available in the finance dashboard.

## Expense Policy
Per diem rates: Domestic travel $75/day meals, International $120/day meals.
Hotel: Up to $250/night domestic, $350/night international (pre-approval required above this).
Flights: Economy class for domestic; business class allowed for flights over 6 hours.
Alcohol is never reimbursable on company expenses.

## Accounts Receivable
Client invoices are generated by the finance team upon project milestone completion.
Outstanding invoices over 60 days are escalated to the client's account manager.
Disputes must be raised within 30 days of invoice date.

## Financial Reporting
Monthly P&L reports are shared with department heads on the 5th of each month.
Annual audit happens in Q1. All departments must complete financial close by January 15th.
Variance explanations over 10% of budget must be submitted to finance.

## Contractor & Freelancer Payments
All contractors must have a signed agreement and W-9/W-8 on file before work begins.
Contractor invoices are paid Net 15.
Payments over $600/year require 1099 filing; finance handles this automatically.
