Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Solace ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it when you use the Solace platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account data: your name, work email address, and hashed password when you register.
  • Usage data: tickets submitted, actions taken within the platform, and session metadata.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information collected automatically via server logs.
  • Uploaded content: documents you upload to the Onboarding AI knowledge base.

We do not collect payment information directly — all billing is handled by our payment provider.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Solace platform.
  • To route and classify support tickets using AI.
  • To generate automated response drafts on your behalf.
  • To send transactional communications related to your account.
  • To monitor platform security and detect abuse.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted on AWS infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. We apply industry-standard access controls and conduct regular security reviews. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: infrastructure and AI API providers (e.g. NVIDIA NIM) that process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities: when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Cookies

Solace uses session cookies strictly necessary for authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@solace.app.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Ticket data is retained for 24 months by default. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of Solace after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions? Contact our privacy team at privacy@solace.app.
